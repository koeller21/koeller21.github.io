School data in Germany

Centralising the decentralised

I recently stumbled upon an issue as I was building a web-service for a start-up company one of my pals founded.

We had to create this sign-up html form for school students where they would enter their school's name so we could later match them with vacation courses taking place at their school.

One could argue that there are conceptually simpler ways of matching students with nearby courses as a student could just get a list of all future courses taking place and then look for their respective school at sign-up. However, we wanted to persist a many-to-one relationship between students and schools in the database for multiple reasons.

For consistency reasons, we also decided we wanted to allow the students only choose from a pre-selected list of schools, i.e. no free-text-form for the school's name at sign-up should be allowed.

This, of course, poses the challenge of having a complete, somewhat homogenous and up-to-date list of all schools in Germany.

Naturally, that complete list of Germany's schools does not exist. It does not exist not because Germany authorities lack the innovative mindset or skills to provide such a list but because Germany's education system is decentralized so each German state provides their own list. This simply reflects the political system in Germany.

Surely it'd be nice if there was a central, free-of-charge repository containing all of Germany's school data such as school name, school address etc. in a single .csv, .json or .xlsx file.

I created such a single, free-of-charge file containing data on all schools in the 16 german states. It is MIT-licsensed.

While creating it I learned about jedeschule.de who nicely visualised a lot of school data and made their cleaned data available for free. The issue being that some of their data is somewhat heterogenous (e.g. school adress sometimes contains city name and sometimes it doesn't) as they provide a separate file for each german state. So their data would likely require further processing.
