Who speaks for earth?

And the world impoverished itself by spending a trillion dollars a year [2022: >$2 trillion] on preparations for war, and by employing perhaps half the scientists and high-technologists on the planet in military endeavours.

How would we explain all this to a dispassionate, extraterrestrial observer?

What account would we give of our stewardship of the planet earth?

We have heard the rationales offered by the superpowers. We know who speaks for the nations.

But who speaks for the human species, who speaks for earth?

- Carl Sagan, 1980.
